About Us

On the day we were founded, we set out with the claim of being "Fenerbahçe's Bright Future" and we have been following this fire ever since. The goals and values of 1907 UNIFEB emerged when we compiled the principles that we believe in, which never leave us, who are members of 1907 UNIFEB alone, on the way to the sacred purpose for all of us, fuel the fire within us in difficult times, and always guide our decisions regarding 1907 UNIFEB. We think that it should be our primary duty to read and reflect on these principles, which we all know very well and believe to apply, and to convey them to our new friends. Thanks again to everyone who showed their hearts for Fenerbahçe's Bright Future.

1907 UNIFEB Mission:
"To provide added value to Fenerbahçe Sports Club, which we support, to the environment and society we live in"
